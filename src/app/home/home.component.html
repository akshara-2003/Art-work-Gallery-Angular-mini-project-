Welcome to ART INSTITVTE CHICAGO

Our Collection

The Art Institute of Chicago houses one of the largest and most significant collections of art in the world. Our collection spans thousands of years and includes works from diverse cultures around the globe.

Exhibitions

Experience our exciting exhibitions, showcasing the very best in art from all corners of the world. From ancient artifacts to contemporary art, we have something for everyone.

Events & Programs

Join us for a wide range of events and programs, including lectures, workshops, tours, and more. Our events are designed to engage, inform, and inspire visitors of all ages.
